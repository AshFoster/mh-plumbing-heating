About Us

A friendly and professional service with amazing attention to detail allowing you to create the bathroom of your dreams. With over 40 years experience, if you require advice on the best approach or design ideas, we can help.


Starting as an apprentice straight after school and working with a local trader, Martin honed his skills and the techniques essential for the job. Once his apprenticeship was complete he then moved on to create his own business with the support of his wife who acts as his secretary. Martin developed his own style and became known for being a perfectionist, regulalry being called on to help with developments in the local area.


Martin has a good rapport with other local trades people and often works alongside them to develop properties to high specifications. Working tidily and methodically there is no worry that your property is in good hands.
