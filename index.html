About Us

A friendly and professional service, with amazing attention to detail allowing you to create the bathroom of your dreams. With over 40 years experience if you require advice on the best approach or design ideas, we can help.


Starting as an appretice straight after school and working with a local trader, I honed the skills and techniques that are essential for this job. Once my apprenticeship was complete I then moved on to create my own business with my wife supporting me and acting as my secretary. I developed my own style and became known for being a perfectionist, regulalry being called on to help with developments in the local area.


I have good repoire with other local trades people and often work alongside them to develop your property to high specifications. Working tidily and methodically there is no worry that your property is in good hands.
